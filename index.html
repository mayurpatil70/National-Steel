About National Steel

National Steel is a well-established steel supplier based in Amalner, Jalgaon, Maharashtra. For over 50 years, we have been a reliable source of premium quality steel products for builders, contractors, fabricators, and individual customers across the region.

We stock a wide range of steel products — from Sintex Water Tanks and Galvanized Corrugated Sheets, Polycarbonate Sheets, and Structural Steel — all sourced from certified manufacturers to ensure top grade quality and value for every rupee you spend.
